Greek Style Garlic Chicken Breast

Cook : 30mins

Additional: 30 mins

Total:1hr

Prep:15 mins

Servings:2

This recipe was passed down through my family for years. If you're looking for a great tasting, tender chicken breast, this is what you're looking for - Greek style.

Ingredients:

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ½ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 1 teaspoons crushed garlic
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon black pepper
  • ⅙ teaspoon paprika

Step 1: Cut 3 slits in each chicken breast to allow marinade to penetrate. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper, and paprika for about 30 seconds. Place chicken in a large bowl, and pour marinade over. Using your hands, work marinade into chicken. Cover, and refrigerate overnight.

Step 2:Preheat grill for medium heat, and lightly oil grate.

Step 3:Cook chicken on grill until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear.
